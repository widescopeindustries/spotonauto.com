**The "Hardware-First" Differentiator:**
"Unlike generic AI mechanic chatbots that rely 100% on user descriptions (which are often vague or inaccurate), SpotOnAuto bridges the physical gap by connecting directly to the vehicle's hardware. Using the browser's Web Serial API, we interface with ELM327 OBD-II scanners to pull **live sensor data and confirmed error codes** in real-time.

This grounds our AI diagnosis in hard data, preventing hallucinations. We then close the loop by generating interactive, step-by-step repair 'stories' (not static text walls) and instantly comparing part prices across major retailers like RockAuto and AutoZone. It is a complete, utility-first ecosystem for DIY repair, not just another wrapper around an LLM."
