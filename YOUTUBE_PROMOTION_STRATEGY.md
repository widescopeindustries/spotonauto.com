## ðŸ“ Ad Formats

### 1. Skippable In-Stream Ads (The "Pre-Roll")
- Plays before other videos.
- User can skip after 5 seconds.
- **Strategy:** Make the first 5 seconds count! "Stop paying mechanics $400!"
- **Call-to-Action:** "Get Free Guide" overlay button.

### 2. In-Feed Video Ads (Discovery)
- Appears in YouTube search results and "Watch Next".
- **Strategy:** Use a thumbnail with big text like "Free AI Mechanic vs $150/hr Shop".
- **Headline:** "Fix Any Car Yourself (Free AI Guide)"

## ðŸ’° Budget & Scaling

### Phase 1: Testing ($10-20/day)
- Run for 5-7 days.
- Test 2-3 different hooks/thumbnails.
- Measure **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**. Benchmark: >1.5%.

### Phase 2: Optimization
- Kill keywords/placements with low CTR.
- Increase bid on high-performing segments.
- Retarget users who visited the site but didn't convert (if pixel installed).

### Phase 3: Scaling ($50+/day)
- Expand to broader auto audiences.
- create variations of the ad for specific car brands ("BMW Owners: Stop Overpaying", "Honda Owners: DIY Fixes").
