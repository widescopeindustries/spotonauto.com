## Website Description

AllOEMManuals is an AI-powered auto repair platform that democratizes factory service information for DIY mechanics and everyday car owners. We combine OEM procedures, diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs), wiring diagrams, torque specs, and step-by-step repair guides into one free, intuitive workspace.

**What makes us different:** Every guide is grounded in actual manufacturer data — not forum guesses or YouTube tutorials. Our AI diagnosis chat links symptoms to DTC codes and routes users to the exact repair procedure for their specific vehicle year, make, and model.
